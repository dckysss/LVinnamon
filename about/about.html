HISTORY

LVinnamon is a cinnamon roll business committed to crafting irresistible and joyful cinnamon rolls that awaken the senses.

From humble beginnings, baking in a small kitchen, LVinnamon quickly gained popularity for its exceptional quality and delightful flavors.

Today, LVinnamon remains a beloved destination for cinnamon roll enthusiasts, delivering the highest quality rolls that bring happiness and comfort in every bite.

Who We Are

At LVinnamon, we are passionate about creating cinnamon rolls that exceed expectations and leave a lasting impression. Our team of talented bakers and pastry chefs work tirelessly to craft each roll with love and care, using only the finest ingredients. We believe that every bite should be a moment of pure indulgence and satisfaction.

Join us on our journey as we continue to spread the love for cinnamon rolls and create unforgettable moments of sweetness and delight. At LVinnamon, we believe that life is better with a cinnamon roll in hand!
